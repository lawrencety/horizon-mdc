Why We're Here

We believe in getting things done.

After years of working closely with next-gen med-tech startups and global companies such as Johnson & Johnson, Stryker, and Cardinal Health, consultants and partners at Horizon realized that complex regulatory strategies can be simplified and that implementation can be streamlined.

We saw that often times, the smoothest way of getting a product to market and keeping it there requires not only deep regulatory knowledge, but also a broader understanding of business strategy and cross-functional collaboration.

We knew it would take a different kind of approach to help companies get to market faster and maintain compliance cost-efficiently. That is why we founded Horizon Medical Device Consulting.
